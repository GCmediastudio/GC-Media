The Vision

Photography started for me through a simple moment of curiosity. After watching my brother capture the world through his lens, I picked up his camera one day and instantly felt a connection. From that moment, I knew this was more than a hobby—it was my future.

Today, GC Media is defined by a commitment to professional, experimental, and creative storytelling. I don't just take pictures; I look for the angles that haven't been seen and the moments that feel raw and authentic.

Based in the Capital Region of New York, I work locally but I am always ready to fly out for global bookings. Wherever the story is, I'm there to capture it.
